Directions to Dave's House in Los Altos (Hills, that is):

From the East Bay

  1. Take I-880 South for a long long time.
  2. Exit to 84 West/Dumbarton Bridge.
  3. Turn left at the 2nd light after the bridge (Willow) and go about a mile.
  4. Get on 101 South (towards San Jose) and go about 3.5 miles.
  5. Exit San Antonio Rd. South/Los Altos and go about 3 miles.
  6. Turn right on Edith. (Make a 90-degree right; a 45-degree right puts you on Main St.)
  7. Cross Foothill Expressway, and you'll shortly come to a T. Turn left on Fremont and then immediately right on Robleda.
  8. Take the first right (Cicerone Lane). Our house is the huge one at the top of the hill, on the right. The address is 13880, but it's not posted anywhere.

If you miss Edith, you'll eventually hit Foothill. Turn right on Foothill and then left on Edith.

From San Francisco

  1. Take I-280 South for a long long time.
  2. Exit Page Mill Road. Make a left (east) at the stop sign and go about a mile.
  3. Turn right (south) at Foothill Expressway. Go about 3 miles.
  4. Turn right at Edith (the third light). You will soon come to a T.
  5. At the T, turn left on Fremont and then immediately right on Robleda.
  6. Take the first right (Cicerone Lane). Our house is the huge one at the top of the hill, on the right. The address is 13880, but it's not posted anywhere.
