🦐 Tsukiji Outer Market
Most stalls close by 1pm so 9am is still plenty early. Graze your way through: fresh sushi, tamagoyaki egg rolls, grilled scallops, uni on rice, oysters, tuna skewers. Most fresh seafood is naturally GF. Easy walk (~15 min) or one stop on the Hibiya Line from Ginza.
